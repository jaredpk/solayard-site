04 — Brand Voice

Voice & Messaging

Warm, confident, and grounded. SolaYard sells outdoor living first and energy second — lead with the lifestyle, back it with real numbers.

Aspirational

Lead with the backyard people want — beautiful structures that happen to make power. Lifestyle first.

Effortless

Emphasize "plug in." No rooftop project, no utility approval, no decades-long contract. Simplicity is the product.

Grounded

Back claims with real rates and payback ranges. Premium, never hype. Trust is earned with numbers.

05 — Application

Usage Guidelines

How to apply the brand consistently across web, print, decks, and email.

✓ Do

  • Use the core palette; gold is the single hero accent
  • Switch to Lustre Gold for the mark on navy
  • Keep generous clear space around the logo
  • Lead with lifestyle, support with real numbers
  • Use Montserrat for headings, Source Sans 3 for body
  • Keep gold for emphasis — small doses, high impact

✕ Don't

  • Recolor, rotate, or distort the panel mark
  • Use core Bronze-Gold #D4A017 on dark navy (too muddy)
  • Set the wordmark in any font but Montserrat
  • Add a second accent color or gradients
  • Use hyped or exaggerated savings claims
  • Place the logo on busy or low-contrast photos
